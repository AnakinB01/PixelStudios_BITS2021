RESPAWN

If you love a challenge, then this one is for you.

Respawn is a pixel art theme based 2D Platformer game.
The difficulty of this game is beyond imaginable. A game with thrilling
background music and never seen animations, a true challenger to all the beast
games out there.

Respawn

~ ABOUT GAME ~

He never expected the twist in his life, he would have never guessed that a nice walk in the park will become a endless run back home. The obstacle he faces in this endless journey only get difficulty, and the path only gets tougher and he has no choice but to continue find his way out. What do you think? Will he find his way home or will this endless journey take him far away from his home!!

Two Contrasting Worlds!! One which accommodates him and accepts him,
where all his friends and family reside.


The other which wants to keep him for endless number of years!! Where
every step of the way an enemy awaits him, and death doesn't accept him. The
only choice he has is to fight!!
